What are the causes of the Gen Z employment Crisis

Causes

  • Automation and AI replacing entry-level jobs:
    Many simple and routine roles that young workers used to start with are now being automated, leaving fewer opportunities for Gen Z to get their first job.
  • Higher job requirements from employers:
    Employers often expect candidates to already have skills and experience, even for entry-level positions, which makes it harder for Gen Z to get started.
  • Economic uncertainty and high living costs:
    Rising rent, food prices, and unstable job markets make it harder for Gen Z to find and keep stable employment.
  • Mismatch between education and job market needs:
    Many Gen Z graduates find that what they studied doesn’t match what employers are actually looking for.
